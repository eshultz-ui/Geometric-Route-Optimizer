Even workload balances each crew's drive time so they finish together. Equal stops gives every crew about the same number of jobs. Fewest miles minimizes total travel and may leave a crew unused. You can enter any number of crews (up to the number of stops).

Cost Inputs

Each crew's route includes the drive out and back. The buffer adds there-and-back minutes per crew (loading, parking). People per crew multiplies travel labor — everyone on the crew is paid for the travel time. Labor = total crew time × rate × people. Gas = total miles ÷ MPG × price/gallon (per vehicle, unaffected by headcount).
